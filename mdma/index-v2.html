The Molecule

MDMA, or 3,4-Methylenedioxymethamphetamine, is a synthetic psychoactive drug that produces a sense of euphoria, empathy, and emotional openness.

History and Research

MDMA was first synthesized in 1912 and later rediscovered in the 1970s for its potential therapeutic applications in psychotherapy.
